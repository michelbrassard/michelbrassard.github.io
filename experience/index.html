I worked as an intern at Laus CC as a part of the ISTE-499 cooperative education course to fulfill my bachelor's degree requirements (800 work hours for which 400 were completed at Laus CC).

Description

During the second year of my bachelor's degree at RIT Croatia, I was employed as a teaching assistant for the ISTE-140 Web and Mobile 1 course. It is an introductory web development course where students learn the basics of technologies such as HTML, CSS, JS, and topics regarding web page design, user experience, intellectual property and website publishing.

For the most part I was there to help students with small issues (usually something in the code doesn't work) or to give them tips for exercises. Other than that, I had to have office hours for any questions, and to be present during exams.
